Meet the maker

A quiet studio,
a single thread,
a lifetime of love.

Kuki's Stitch is a brand I created. I'm a senior citizen, and I learned this craft from my mum — an art that is slowly disappearing. Decades later, that same gentle craft has become my way of bringing small joys into the world — one little character at a time.

Every toy is made by hand from start to finish, in a slow, deliberate rhythm that no machine could ever replicate. I use only premium organic cotton, hypoallergenic stuffing, and embroidered details, so each companion is safe to be loved hard, washed gently, and kept forever.

And I'd love to teach — I can train anyone who is interested in learning this beautiful art.

How It's Made

From sketchbook to your doorstep.

No two pieces are ever quite the same. Here's how each little companion comes to life.

i

Design

Every character begins as a pencil sketch. We choose colours together, plan each detail, and create a one-of-one pattern just for you.

ii

Crochet

Stitch by gentle stitch, your companion takes shape. We use only organic cotton, hand-embroidered features, and hypoallergenic filling — never glue.

iii

Ship with care

Each piece is wrapped in tissue paper, tied with cotton ribbon, and tucked into a keepsake box — ready to be opened and loved.
